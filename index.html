How to Play

Follow these steps to learn about the labyrinth.

You'll be given 6x6 (1 to 6) Horizontally, (A to F) Vertically grids with borders between them.

Fill in 20 boxes and set Starting and Ending points for your opponent.

Then Click on 'OK' then wait for your opponent.

Then you'll be given another 6x6 box with hidden borders.

Then you got to start from starting point to ending point set by your opponent, your opponent will do the same.

Each time you cross the wall, turn will be switched.

Whoever reaches the end point first will be victorious.
